Frequently Asked Questions

What types of generators do you offer?

We offer a wide range of generators including diesel, petrol, standby, portable, and industrial generators.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our generator products.

What maintenance services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive maintenance services including routine inspections, oil changes, filter replacements, and load testing.

Are your generators suitable for residential use?

Yes, we offer generators that are suitable for both residential and commercial use. Our team can help you select the right generator based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we understand that emergencies can occur at any time. We offer 24/7 emergency repair services to ensure that your power needs are met promptly.

What brands of generators do you carry?

We carry generators from top brands such as Perkins, Cummins, SDMO, Deutz, Volvo Penta, and many more. Our selection includes high-quality generators known for their reliability and performance.

Can you provide custom power solutions for specific needs?

Absolutely! We specialize in providing tailored power solutions to meet the unique needs of our clients. Whether you require a custom generator configuration or a specialized power system, we can design and deliver a solution that fits your requirements.

How do I determine the right generator size for my needs?

Our team of experts can assist you in determining the appropriate generator size based on factors such as power requirements, load demand, and intended usage. We'll work closely with you to ensure that you select the right generator to meet your needs effectively.

What warranty do you offer on your generators?

We stand behind the quality of our products and offer warranties on all our generators. The warranty duration may vary depending on the specific generator model and brand. Our team will provide you with detailed warranty information for your chosen generator.

How can I request a quote for your services?

You can easily request a quote by contacting our team either through phone, email, or by filling out the contact form on our website. We'll promptly respond to your inquiry and provide you with a competitive quote tailored to your needs.
